ALEJANDRO ALARCÓN

CREATIVE PRODUCER

Wouldn't call myself an artist nor a designer, yet I do both, for others and me.

Creative Producer is a more fitting term because I serve as a conduit for entrepreneurs, builders and artists, helping them shape their own visions into reality, or at least that's what I've been doing for the last 8 years of my 15+ year career as a creative.

But I am also deeply invested on my own stuff. As I write this, I am building what I see as the synthesis of everything I have learned so far; a label for misfits at the fringe, where tech, art and entrepreneurship can meet, with creative sovereignty as the guiding light. It is called T******T F****E and its first stage is set to see the light in the summer of 2025.

I was a fan before a creator, and I still am. Pop culture (probably the darker side of it) and technology—how they shape us—have been a lifelong fascination, and I share humanity's ancient itch for understanding: reality, myself, others—what this "all" means(?).

I have more questions than answers, but one thing I do know, is that I want to see a world with more playgrounds than galleries, where more people are active players rather than passive observers. I believe that interfaces are a vehicle for active experience. In that sense, my current quest is to build the playgrounds of tomorrow.
